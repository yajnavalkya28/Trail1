About This Digital Library

Welcome to our Digital Library project. This platform is designed to provide seamless access to a curated collection of books and electronic resources for registered students and administrators.

Our goal is to create a user-friendly environment where students can easily search for, borrow, and read materials, while administrators can efficiently manage the library's collection, post important announcements, and gather user feedback.

This project utilizes modern web technologies (HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript) and leverages browser `localStorage` for data persistence in this demonstration version.
